Conclusions

  • Dynamic batching gave the best results: up to 1.73 req/s and 140 tok/s vs 0.57 and 31 for baseline.
  • Static batching helped on light/medium but was slower than baseline on heavy.
  • KV cache (use_cache=True) gives ~16–45% higher tok/s; gain grows with sequence length.
